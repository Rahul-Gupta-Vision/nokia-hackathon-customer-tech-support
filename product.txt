SECTION 1: COMPANY OVERVIEW
-----------------------------------------------------------
This is a business unit focused on broadband access, metro aggregation, and service provider routing solutions. Our mission is to deliver carrier-grade networking systems that ensure scalability, reliability, and operational simplicity.

With over a decade of experience in Ethernet, IP/MPLS, and timing technologies, NBN provides a portfolio of products that power broadband networks worldwide. The Product1, Product2, and Product3 are our flagship platforms delivering advanced Layer 2/3 services for residential, enterprise, and 5G backhaul deployments.

-----------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: PRODUCT PORTFOLIO
-----------------------------------------------------------
1. Product1 – High-density access switch optimized for Layer 2 aggregation and QoS.
2. Product2 – Modular fabric platform supporting converged Layer 2/3 and MPLS services.
3. Product3 – Compact router supporting Layer 3, MPLS, EVPN, and timing protocols.

-----------------------------------------------------------
PRODUCT: Product1
- 48x1G SFP ports + 4x10G SFP+ uplinks
- Switching capacity: 176 Gbps
- Latency: <1.2µs (L2 forwarding)
- Power: 180W typical
- MTBF: 280,000 hours
- Operating Temp: 0–50°C

PRODUCT: Product2

- Dual control plane processors

PRODUCT: Product3
- 16x10G + 4x40G uplinks
- Hardware-assisted routing ASIC
- MPLS and SRv6 acceleration in silicon
- Power: 350W typical
- Redundant power and fan modules
- Dimensions: 2RU

-----------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4: SOFTWARE CAPABILITIES
-----------------------------------------------------------
All platforms run on the NBN Network OS v3.x framework.

CORE CAPABILITIES
- Dual-stack IPv4/IPv6
- VLAN (802.1Q), QinQ (802.1ad), and VLAN translation
- STP/RSTP/MSTP support
- LACP (802.3ad) with fast failover
- MPLS L3VPN/L2VPN and EVPN
- Segment Routing (SR-MPLS, SRv6)
- QoS with hierarchical scheduling and shaping
- DHCP server, relay, and snooping (v4/v6)
- PTP IEEE 1588v2 and SyncE
- CFM (802.1ag) and Y.1731 OAM
- MACsec encryption on user and uplink ports
- NETCONF/YANG, RESTCONF, and gNMI telemetry
- Syslog, TACACS+, and RADIUS integration

SECTION 6: USE CASES
-----------------------------------------------------------
1. **Enterprise Ethernet Service Assurance** — Using CFM/Y.1731 to monitor SLA KPIs.
2. **Access Aggregation Redundancy** — LACP for link aggregation and failover.
3. **Broadband IP Allocation** — DHCPv4/v6 relay and option-82 insertion.
4. **MPLS L3VPN** — Multi-tenant separation for enterprise customers.
5. **Data Center Interconnect** — EVPN-VXLAN for Layer 2 extension.
6. **5G Backhaul** — SR-MPLS and PTP for synchronization.
7. **Metro Aggregation** — QoS prioritization for IPTV and VoIP.
